User Guidelines

Learn how to make the most of JomGather and connect with fellow MMU students!

🚀 Getting Started

1. Create Your Account

Click "Sign Up/Log In" from the homepage
Fill in your MMU student details (Student ID, Name, Email)
Create a secure password and verify your email

2. Complete Your Profile

Navigate to "Customize Profile" in the top menu
Upload a profile picture and add your interests
Select your faculty, year, and semester
Write a brief bio to introduce yourself
💡 Tip: A complete profile helps you find better matches and makes others more likely to connect with you!

👥 Finding & Connecting with Students

Using the Match System

Go to "Jom Gather" from the main menu
Use the "Match Your Interests" feature to find compatible students
Browse through profiles and click "Connect" on interesting matches
Send a personalized connection request

Finding Study Partners

Use the search filters to find students in your faculty/year
Look for students with similar academic interests
Check their availability and study preferences
🎯 Pro Feature: Use the "Find Hangout Partners" section to discover students who share your hobbies and interests outside of academics!

🎉 Creating & Joining Gatherings

Creating Your Own Gathering

Go to "Jom Gather" and click "Create Gathering"
Choose gathering type (Study Session, Social Event, Sports, etc.)
Set date, time, location, and maximum participants
Write a clear description of what to expect
Specify your target audience (faculty, year level, interests)

Joining Existing Gatherings

Browse available gatherings in the "Discover Events" section
Read the description and requirements carefully
Click "Join" if you're interested and meet the criteria
Wait for the organizer's approval
💡 Tip: Be respectful of gathering requirements and show up on time if you commit to attending!

💬 Communication & Chat

Messaging Connected Students

Once a connection is accepted, you can start chatting
Access chats through your accepted connections
Be friendly and respectful in all communications
Share your contact details only when comfortable

Gathering Group Chats

Join gathering-specific group chats to coordinate with other participants
Use group chat to share updates, ask questions, or get to know other members
Keep conversations relevant to the gathering purpose

🔒 Safety & Best Practices

Profile Safety

Only share information you're comfortable with being public
Use your real name and student ID for authenticity
Report any suspicious or inappropriate behavior

Meeting in Person

Always meet new connections in public, well-lit areas
Inform a friend about your plans when meeting someone new
Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, prioritize your safety
🛡️ Safety First: JomGather is designed to be a safe space for MMU students. Help us maintain this by being respectful and reporting any concerns.

⚙️ Managing Your Experience

Privacy Settings

Review and update your profile visibility preferences
Control who can send you connection requests
Manage your notification settings

Account Management

Regularly update your interests and availability
Keep your contact information current
Remove or block users who make you uncomfortable
🎨 Customization: Make your profile stand out by regularly updating your interests, adding new photos, and keeping your bio fresh!

🤝 Community Guidelines

Be respectful and inclusive in all interactions
Keep conversations appropriate and friendly
Honor your commitments to gatherings and study sessions
Help create a welcoming environment for all MMU students
Report any violations of community standards
🌟 Remember: JomGather is all about building positive connections and creating meaningful experiences. Be the kind of person you'd want to connect with!
